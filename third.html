Visitor Tips

Tips for a Great Experience

Welcome to Wonderland Park! Here are some insider tips to help first-time visitors enjoy a memorable and hassle-free experience:

  • Plan Ahead: Review the park map and schedule in advance to prioritize attractions and shows.
  • Arrive Early: Beat the crowds and enjoy cooler weather in the morning.
  • Comfortable Attire: Wear comfortable clothing and shoes suitable for walking and weather conditions.
  • Stay Hydrated: Bring a refillable water bottle to stay hydrated throughout the day.
  • Use Fast Passes: Take advantage of fast passes for popular attractions to save time.
  • Check Height Requirements: If visiting with children, check ride height requirements ahead of time.
  • Rest and Recharge: Take breaks at designated relaxation zones in the park to rest and recharge.
  • Explore Dining Options: Try out various food courts and restaurants for different culinary experiences.
  • Be Prepared for Weather: Bring sunscreen, hats, or rain gear depending on the forecast.

  • Use Locker Facilities: Rent a locker to safely store personal belongings and souvenirs.
  • Keep an Eye on Children: Always supervise children and consider using a buddy system.
  • Ask for Assistance: Park staff are always available to help or provide information.
  • Download the Park App: Use the park's mobile app for real-time updates and interactive maps.
  • Enjoy the Moments: Most importantly, relax and enjoy the magical moments in the park!


FAQ

What are the park opening hours?

Our park is open from 9 AM to 8 PM, seven days a week.

Do you offer any group discounts?

Yes, we offer special rates for groups of 15 or more. Please contact our group sales department for more details.

Are there any height or age restrictions for the rides?

Yes, some rides have height and age restrictions for safety. These are listed at the entrance of each ride and on our website.

Is outside food allowed in the park?

Outside food is not permitted, but we have a wide variety of dining options available inside the park.

What should I do if I lose an item during my visit?

Lost items are taken to our lost and found department, located near the park entrance. You can also contact our guest services for assistance.

Are pets allowed in the park?

Pets are not permitted, except for service animals.

Do you have facilities for guests with disabilities?

Yes, we offer a range of services for guests with disabilities, including accessible rides and attractions, rental wheelchairs, and more.

Can I re-enter the park if I leave during the day?

Yes, you can re-enter the park on the same day with a hand stamp or your ticket.

Are there any accommodations for overnight stays?

We have partnered hotels nearby offering comfortable accommodations. You can find more information on our website.

Do you have a map of the park?

Yes, park maps are available at the entrance and various locations throughout the park. You can also download the map from our website.

What measures are in place for guest safety and security?

We prioritize guest safety with trained security personnel, comprehensive safety protocols for rides, and health services on site.

How can I purchase tickets?

Tickets can be purchased online through our website or at the park entrance.
