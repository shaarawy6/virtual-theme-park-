Welcome to Wonderland Park
Discover thrilling rides, fun shows, and unforgettable adventures for all. Start your magical journey today!



Wonderland Park Map



Activities

Water Rides

Dive into the aquatic paradise of 'Splash Kingdom', our water wonderland! Here, excitement meets relaxation with a variety of water slides ranging from thrilling high-speed plunges to gentle, meandering flows. Enjoy the massive wave pools that mimic the ocean's rhythms, or unwind in the lazy river that encircles this watery oasis. With something for everyone, 'Splash Kingdom' is your go-to destination for fun in the sun and the ultimate way to cool off and make a splash!

Roller Coasters

Get ready for heart-racing excitement at 'Thrill Mountain'! Our roller coasters offer the ultimate adrenaline rush with towering drops, swift turns, and breathtaking speeds. From the classic wooden coasters to the modern steel giants, each ride is a thrilling adventure. Buckle up and prepare to scream your way through the twists and turns of our most exhilarating attractions!

Merry-Go-Round

Step into the charming realm of 'Carousel Cove' and take a whimsical ride on our classic merry-go-round. Adorned with beautifully crafted horses and fantastical creatures, it's a magical experience for the young and young at heart. The enchanting music and gentle rotation create a nostalgic atmosphere perfect for creating lasting memories with your loved ones.

Game Booths

Try your luck and skill at 'Arcade Alley', a carnival-style stretch of game booths filled with chances to win prizes. Test your aim with ring tosses, knock down towers in ball throws, or step into the batting cages. With games of chance and skill, there's endless fun for everyone. Step right up and win the prize of the day!

Food Courts

Sample a wide range of foods, from fast snacks to gourmet treats at our park's food courts. Indulge in flavors from around the world, catered to satisfy every palate. Whether you're looking for a quick bite or a leisurely dining experience, our food courts offer the perfect spot to refuel and relax.

Gardens

Take a leisurely stroll through beautifully landscaped gardens. Immerse yourself in the tranquility of nature, admire the artistry of our garden designers, and perhaps even catch a butterfly or two flitting amongst the flowers.

Shows and Performances

Watch live entertainment such as parades, musical performances, and character meet-and-greets. Our daily lineup of shows brings endless smiles to faces of all ages, ensuring that every moment at the park is filled with wonder and delight.

Shopping

Browse through souvenir shops for memorabilia, clothing, and unique gifts. Take a piece of the park home with you with our exclusive merchandise, perfect for remembering your day of fun.

Relaxation Zones

Unwind in designated quiet areas or enjoy a picnic in park-provided seating areas. Find your moment of peace in the midst of excitement.

Arcades

Play the latest video games or enjoy classic arcade games in the gaming zone. Challenge friends to a game or set a new high score in this electrifying arcade atmosphere.

Adventure Trails

For the more adventurous, explore obstacle courses or climbing structures. Test your limits and enjoy the thrill of adventure in our action-packed trails.

Seasonal Events

Participate in seasonal events like Halloween haunts, Christmas markets, or summer festivals. Our park transforms with the seasons, offering new and exciting experiences year-round.



Events

Summer Spectacular

Dive into our 'Summer Spectacular' this July, a celebration that transforms the park into a vibrant festival of joy and excitement! Every weekend, we bring you an array of special performances, ranging from live music concerts to breathtaking acrobatic displays. Enjoy the energetic dance shows, interactive street performances, and family-friendly entertainment that fill the air with laughter and wonder. It's more than just fun; it's an explosion of culture and festivity under the summer sun, creating memories that last a lifetime.

Halloween Haunt

Dare to enter the spine-tingling realm of our Halloween event this October! Immerse yourself in a world where haunted houses rise from the shadows, offering a chilling adventure at every corner. Wander through corridors filled with eerie surprises and encounter ghostly inhabitants that lurk in the dark. Our frightful shows will captivate you with tales of the supernatural, where performers bring legends and spooky stories to life. It's a mesmerizing experience that blends scares with theatrical artistry, perfect for those who love a good thrill!

Winter Wonderland

Step into a magical 'Winter Wonderland' this December, where the park is transformed into a festive paradise. Stroll through a sparkling landscape of lights and decorations, enjoy classic holiday treats, and take part in joyous carol singing. Meet your favorite winter characters, from jolly Santa Claus to playful elves, and capture these special moments with your loved ones. It's a heartwarming celebration of the festive season, filled with cheer, warmth, and delightful surprises around every corner.



Festivals

International Food and Wine Fair

Celebrate culinary delights from around the world. The park transforms into a global village with food stalls and wine tasting booths. Indulge in an exquisite selection of international cuisines and wines, enhancing your gastronomic experience.

  • International food stalls
  • Wine and cheese tastings
  • Cooking demonstrations
  • Cultural performances

Family Fun Fair

A family-oriented festival with activities suitable for all ages. The park is filled with extra fun activities and shows designed for family enjoyment. Join us for a day of laughter, joy, and unforgettable family moments.

  • Carnival games
  • Family-friendly shows
  • Magic performances
  • Clown acts
  • Face painting
